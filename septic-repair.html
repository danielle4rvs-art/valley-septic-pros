Repair Questions

How do I know if my drain field is failing?

Sewage odors that won't quit, standing water or mushy ground over the leach lines, and backups even after pumping. A failing field needs a real diagnosis — sometimes it's repairable, sometimes it's not. We'll tell you which, straight.

Sewage is backing up into my house right now. What do I do?

Stop running water immediately — no flushing, no laundry, no dishes. Then call us. An emergency pump-out usually relieves the backup the same day while we find the cause.

Will you try to sell me a new system I don't need?

No. Most problems we see are fixed with pumping, a baffle repair, or root removal for a fraction of replacement cost. If replacement truly is the answer, we'll show you why.
