Is It Time? Check These Signs

  • It's been 3+ years since your last pumping (or you can't remember)
  • Slow drains or gurgling toilets anywhere in the house
  • Sewage smell in the yard, or a soggy / extra-green patch over the tank
  • You've got a big household, a garbage disposal, or lots of laundry days

Any one of these? Call now — pumping today is a few hundred dollars. A backup next month is a whole different story.

Pumping Questions

What's included in a pumping?

Complete pump-out of solids and liquids (not just the liquid layer), a baffle and inlet/outlet check, and a condition report. Some outfits skim the top and leave the sludge — we don't.

How long does it take?

Usually 45–90 minutes once we're on-site, depending on tank size and access.

Do I need to find my tank lid first?

No. If you don't know where your tank is, we'll locate it. It helps to know roughly where, but it's not required.
