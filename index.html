Questions Homeowners Ask Us

How often should I pump my septic tank?

Every 3 to 5 years for most homes. Big household, garbage disposal, or an older tank? Lean toward 3. If you can't remember your last pumping, it's time.

How much does septic pumping cost around Nuevo?

Most standard pumpings in the Perris Valley run $400–$650 depending on tank size and access. You get the exact price before any work starts — never after.

My drains are slow and there's a smell outside. Is that my septic?

Very likely, yes. Slow drains, gurgling toilets, odors, or soggy spots over the tank are the classic warning signs. Call before it backs up into the house — that's when it gets expensive.

Do you work on horse properties and larger lots?

Every day. Long driveways, rear-lot tanks, and gated properties are normal out here. Just tell us about access when you call.
